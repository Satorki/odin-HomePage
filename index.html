Kira

About

Meet Kira, a delightful 9-year-old Border Collie with a striking black and white coat. Kira is the epitome of a happy and content dog, radiating a serene and gentle demeanor. She is notably quiet and well-mannered, making her a wonderful companion for peaceful moments. However, her keen senses come alive at the sight of cats or perceived threats, causing her to bark and alert her family. Kira's intelligence and loyalty shine through her every action, making her a beloved member of her household.
